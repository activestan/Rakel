Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize commercial transportation in Nigeria, Rakel Autos has grown to become a leading importer and distributor of high-quality Japanese mini buses and shuttle vehicles.

With deep roots in the automotive industry and a passion for reliable mobility solutions, we source only the best accident-free, fuel-efficient vehicles directly from Japan. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of businesses, schools, churches, and transport operators across the country.

From Lagos to Abuja, Port Harcourt to Kano, we deliver vehicles that are built to withstand Nigerian roads while providing comfort, durability, and low operating costs.
